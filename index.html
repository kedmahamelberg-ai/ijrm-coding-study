How the coding interface works

For each post, you will answer 5 questions. Question 1 is a simple gate question:

Q1: Is this post clearly spam, off-topic, or non-English?
  • Yes — pick this only if the post is unreadable to you (non-English without translation, gibberish, raw URLs only) OR is obviously promotional spam with no substantive content about AI. The other 4 questions will be answered automatically and you move on.
  • No — pick this if you can read the post and there's any AI-related observation in it, even if it's marketing-flavored. The other 4 questions will appear so you can code it.

If you answer "No" to Q1, you will then assign codes for:

  • Consumer Experience (Q2) — what is the human in the post experiencing?
  • AI Role (Q4) — what is the AI doing?
  • Plus your confidence for each (Q3 and Q5).

The full coding manual is one click away from any session — look for the 📖 Codebook button at the top of the page.

Visual signals

  • The left edge of each post card turns green when you've fully coded that post.
  • The progress counter at the top of the page shows how many posts you've completed.
  • Your work is saved automatically — you can refresh, close the tab, or come back tomorrow without losing progress.
  • The "Mark Session N as complete" button at the bottom of each session is enabled only when ALL posts in that session show green.
